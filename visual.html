What is BERTopic?

We use custom BERTopic hyperparameter which is indo-sentence-bert-base for the embeddings, KMeans for the clustering, and KeyBERTInspired for the representatin model.

This website is intended for the final project which is made as one of the graduation requirements to obtain a Bachelor of Informatics degree in the Bachelor of Science (S-1) program at the Faculty of Electrical Technology and Intelligent Informatics, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember.
